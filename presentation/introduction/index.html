Watercoolr

The problem:

"Sometimes you are too busy to be able to attend/watch/listen to something live"

You might be doing something else or maybe you were too hungover.

"Everyone was talking about it at the time and commenting on it"

They might have been tweeting, posting comments on facebook or just chatting.

"You want to be part of the experience, even though you couldn't be there"

You want to know what you friends were saying as it was happening

"Luckily you can watch it/listen to it later, but the moment has past"

Lets face it, you missed it cause you suck and now your craving to be part of it.

The idea:

What if you could replay the responses as you watch it.


You could add your feedback to the stream.


Other people who are replaying it after you will also see your comments.


What kind of media

Live TV, Sports Events, Concerts

  • If you couldn't attend it, you might be able to watch later (iPlayer, etc)
  • Maybe things were happening that weren't broadcast

Radio shows, Live Podcasts, Broadcasts

  • Some shows encourage participation via hashtags
  • More information is posted than time to address in the show
  • The presenter can clarify certain points recorded live

Presentations and Webinars

  • Most slides end up online
  • (Some) people have relevant feedback or corrections
  • The presenter can clarify certain points recorded live

Technology

Soundcloud Widget - Radio/Podcast Media.


jQuery - Displaying the responses.


Soundcloud API - Collect and existing comments.


Rails backend - Storing, time of event, comments
